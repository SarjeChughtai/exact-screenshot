# Brainstorm Options

**Trigger:** `/brainstorm`  
**Description:** Explore competing approaches before choosing one to implement.

---

## Steps

### 1. Read the minimal context
Read any files directly relevant to the decision.  
Do not read the whole repo unless the choice genuinely depends on broad architecture.

### 2. Define the decision
State:
- the problem to solve
- the constraints
- what success looks like

### 3. Generate options
Provide at least three distinct approaches.  
For each option, include:
- what it changes
- why it is attractive
- why it is risky
- rough effort: low, medium, or high

### 4. Recommend one option
Give a recommendation only after comparing tradeoffs.

### 5. Convert the winner into a next step
End with the exact first action required if the recommended option is chosen.
