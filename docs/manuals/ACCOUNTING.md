# Accounting Manual

## Main tools
- `Payment Ledger`
- `Client Payments`
- `Vendor Payments`
- `Projected Financials`
- `Monthly HST`
- `Commission Statement`
- `Commission & Profit`

## Primary responsibilities
- record money in and money out accurately
- verify tax treatment by province
- monitor client and vendor payment stages
- review profitability and commission outputs

## Standard workflow
1. Enter or update payment transactions in `Payment Ledger`.
2. Review client-side payment status in `Client Payments`.
3. Review vendor-side payment obligations in `Vendor Payments`.
4. Monitor projections in `Projected Financials`.
5. Verify commission reporting in `Commission Statement`.

## Payment Ledger usage
Always verify:
- job ID
- direction
- payment type
- amount excluding tax
- tax rate or override
- reference number

## Common mistakes to avoid
- entering vendor payments against the wrong province or tax treatment
- treating projected values as confirmed cash movement
- editing payment records without checking downstream commission effects
