Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Canada Steel Buildings ("we", "us", "our") operates CSB Tracker. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information, including data accessed through the QuickBooks Online integration.

2. Information We Collect

When you use CSB Tracker and connect to QuickBooks Online, we may access:

  • Account information (name, email, company name)
  • QuickBooks financial data (invoices, customers, payments, items)
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens for maintaining the QuickBooks connection
  • Usage data (pages visited, features used within CSB Tracker)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the CSB Tracker service
  • Sync data between CSB Tracker and your QuickBooks Online account
  • Improve and troubleshoot the application
  • Communicate with you about your account or service updates

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties, except:

  • With Intuit, as required to operate the QuickBooks integration
  • When required by law or legal process
  • With service providers who assist in operating CSB Tracker (e.g., hosting), under strict confidentiality agreements

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and access controls. OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest. We use Supabase for backend services with row-level security enabled.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you disconnect QuickBooks or delete your account, we will remove associated QuickBooks tokens and synced data within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Disconnect QuickBooks at any time through the application settings
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

8. QuickBooks-Specific Disclosures

CSB Tracker accesses QuickBooks data solely to provide its core functionality — syncing invoices, customers, and financial records. We do not use QuickBooks data for advertising, marketing to third parties, or any purpose unrelated to the CSB Tracker service.

9. Cookies & Tracking

CSB Tracker may use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@canadasteelbuildings.ca.
